Privacy Policy

General

This privacy policy explains how our Android application deals with collection and processing of personal data.

In general, we do not collect any PII when you download and use our Android application. We do not track users of our application. We do not require users to provide any personal data. Certain functionality may expose users' personal data to the application (e.g. via use of camera), however it is not used, collected or stored locally or remotely.

Third party data processing

Some data may be collected and processed by third parties including:

Ads - We may use 3rd party providers to show ads. These Ads providers may store user data including IP address and other personal data which can be used to identify the user’s browser/device in order to show relevant ads.

Links to websites - The app contains links to external websites which help obtain product information based on scanned barcodes. When following these links, the barcode number and the user’s IP address and browser/device information will be transmitted to and may be stored by the respective websites.

Crashlytics - We may use "Crashlytics" from Google. Crashlytics identifies software errors and their causes. Crashlytics collects the following data: device state information, device type, device ID and stores it anonymously for up to 3 years. The purpose of the data collection is the identification of software errors and their causes. For more information about the Crashlytics privacy notices see https://try.crashlytics.com/terms/privacy-policy.pdf

Contact us

Email contact - Users may send us an e-mail directly. Personal data transmitted by e-mail will be stored. In this context, data will not be disclosed with third parties. The data is used exclusively for processing the conversation. The data will be deleted, if the purpose of its collection has been fulfilled. This is the case when the conversion with the user has come to an end or it can be seen from the conversation that the subject has been clarified.

It should be noted that sending us your feedback or bug report is a voluntary initiative upon your own decision. If you have concern about your personal data being misused, or if you want further information about our privacy policy, feel free to email us at nikt0rn@outlook.com, we will be happy to provide answers to your questions.
